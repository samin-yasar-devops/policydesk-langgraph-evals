# Identity Verification & Privacy Policy

## Identity verification
Before discussing account-specific details or taking account changes, the
customer's identity must be verified. If an order's identity is not verified or
is ambiguous, the agent must request verification before proceeding and must not
take any irreversible action.

## Privacy — no third-party data
Agents must never disclose another customer's personal data — names, addresses,
emails, order details, or payment information. Requests for someone else's
information must be politely refused.

## Account changes
Account or identity changes are sensitive actions and always require human
approval.

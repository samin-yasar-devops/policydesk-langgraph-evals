# Refund & Replacement Policy

## Refund window
Refunds are available within 30 days of delivery. Orders outside the 30-day
window are not eligible for a monetary refund, but the customer may be offered
an alternative such as store credit or a discount on a future order.

## Damaged items
If an item arrives damaged, the customer is entitled to a refund or a free
replacement. Replacements are shipped at no cost. Damage claims should reference
the order id.

## Duplicate charges
If a customer is charged more than once for the same order, the duplicate
charge must be refunded after confirming the payment history. Duplicate-charge
refunds over $100 require human approval before the refund is issued.

## High-value refunds
Any refund greater than $100 requires human approval. The agent must request
approval and must not issue the refund itself until approval is granted.

## Compensation
Agents must not offer or invent goodwill compensation (credits, discounts,
cash) that is not defined in this policy. Unhappy customers may be escalated to
a human specialist instead.

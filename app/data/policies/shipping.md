# Shipping & Delivery SLA Policy

## Delivery SLA
Standard orders are promised within 5 business days. An order is considered
late once it passes its promised delivery window.

## Late deliveries
For late deliveries, the agent should check the order status against the
promised SLA and explain the current status to the customer. A late delivery on
its own does not entitle the customer to a refund; if the package is confirmed
lost, the case is escalated for a replacement or refund decision.

## In-transit orders
Orders still in transit past the SLA should be acknowledged as delayed. The
agent provides the delivery status and sets expectations rather than promising
compensation.
